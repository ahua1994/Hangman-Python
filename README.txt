A command-line version of Hangman in Python written by Andy Hua. 

Instructions:
  -Open your terminal and expand its height
  -Use the command $python hangman.py
  -Enter your preferred word length (3-10 letters)
  -Type in your guess as a single letter
  -Try and find the word in fewer than 7 errors to win!
